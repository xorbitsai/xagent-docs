Build agents that can create professional presentations, slide decks, and visual content.

## What It Does

A PPT Generation Agent can:
- Create complete slide presentations
- Design professional layouts
- Generate charts and visualizations
- Maintain brand consistency

## How It Works

Describe what you need:

```
"Create a Q3 business review presentation with:
1. Executive summary (2 slides)
2. Revenue performance with charts (3 slides)
3. Key achievements and milestones (2 slides)
4. Q4 outlook and goals (2 slides)
5. Use our brand colors and professional design"
```

Xagent will:
- Structure the content logically
- Design each slide appropriately
- Create visualizations for data
- Apply consistent styling
